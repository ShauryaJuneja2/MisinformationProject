STRATEGIES TO PREVENT GETTING FOOLED

Being able to tell if something is real or fake. This is especially important on the internet, as it can help stop the spread of misinformation. Here are some strategies you can use to double-check your information:

Checking The Date

Sometimes information can be outdated, which can be taken out of context. This is a very common trick that can be used to mislead people.

Investigating The Source

Take a deeper dive into the information at hand, try to search up the information(lateral reading), or look more closely if there is an image or a video.

Fact Checking Sites

Sometimes information can be outdated, which can be taken out of context. These can include government websites, reliable news sources, or myth busting sites.
